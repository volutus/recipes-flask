Grandma Schultz's Pie Crust

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cups shortening

Directions

  1. Combine dry ingredients and mix until integrated
  2. Cut in shortening using knives until shaggy
  3. Mix in water and mix to combine
